Visit the Society of Illustrators!


❅ Located at 128 E. 63rd Street, New York, New York, the Society of Illustrators is a professional organization. In 1901, it was founded to promote illustration and, since 1959, it has held annual exhibitions.

❅ It is open from Wednesday-Saturday. Hours are from 11 am - 5 pm.
