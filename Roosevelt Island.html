Activities at Roosevelt Island

Take the Roosevelt Island Tramway!


❅ Runs every 7-15 minutes from Tramway Plaza on Roosevelt Island to 59th Street and Second Avenue in Manhattan.

❅ The views from the tram are very beautiful and definitely worth experiencing.

❅ Costs the same price as a subway fare ($2.75).

Restaurant in Roosevelt Island

Nisi


549 Main St, New York, NY 10044

❅ Nisi is an American traditional Restaurant. They have different types of seafood, as well as breakfast and brunch.

Transfers from Roosevelt Island

Roosevelt Island Tramway


❅ Tramway in NYC that spans the East River and connects Roosevelt Island to the Upper East Side of Manhattan.

❅ It takes you directly to 59th Street and 2nd Ave where you can catch the F train.
