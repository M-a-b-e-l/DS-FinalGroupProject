Activities at Rockefeller

Tis the season to visit the ice skating rink



❅ 100% of travelers recommend this ice skating rink and the scenery of Rockefeller is very nice.

❅ It is open from November - March, you can bring your own stakes or rent them, but regardless you will have a nice experience


Christmas Tree


❅ Visit the Christmas tree which will remain until January.

❅ The Tree will be lit daily from 6am-12am.

❅ On Christmas Day, the Tree is lit for 24 hours

❅ New Year's Eve it is lit from 6am to 9pm.
